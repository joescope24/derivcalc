About DerivCalc

Hello! I'm Joe Scopelliti, a current graduate student at the University of Maryland Robert H. Smith School of Business.

I intend to graduate with a Master of Science Degree in Quantitative Finance in the Spring of 2024. Recently graduated from the Smith School with a Bachelor's Degree in Finance and Information Systems, I set out with my experience to build this website in my free time.

This project was inspired by one of the courses in my program exploring Financial Mathematics. I found the material very interesting, however I did not feel very comfortable with all the math and coding at the time. My personal goal for building this website was to master the skills needed to price different types of derivatives while simultaneously leveraging my technical background in Information Systems to build a platform that easily and seamlessly enables others to do the same.
